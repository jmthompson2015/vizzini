Grays
This page displays grayscale colors for the RGB color model, which is an additive color model.

Achromatic Grays

Formed with equal RGB values.

Off-Grays

Whose red, green, and blue color codes are not exactly equal.

Cool Grays

Colors that are noticeably blueish gray, grayish cyan, greenish gray, or violetish gray.

Warm Grays

Colors that are noticeably brownish, pinkish grays, or reddish purple grays.
